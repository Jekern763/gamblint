## Maximum Payout Function

Let $G(r)$ be the best guess given r.

$$
G(r)=
\begin{cases}
    r & r \in (-\infty, 5] \cup [9, \infty) \\[1em]
    \frac{r+5}{2}  & r \in [5, 7] \\[1em]
    \frac{r+9}{2}  & r \in [7, 9]
\end{cases}
$$
